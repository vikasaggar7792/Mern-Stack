What are Logical and Physical Tags in HTML?

Logical Tags

Logical tags describe the meaning of the content rather than its appearance. They improve accessibility and SEO.

Examples:

  • <em>
  • <strong>
  • <cite>
  • <code>
  • <address>

Physical Tags

Physical tags define how the content should appear visually in the browser.

Examples:

  • <b>
  • <i>
  • <u>
  • <big>
  • <small>
