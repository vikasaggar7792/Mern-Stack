Question 100

How Will You Implement That When You Click on the Submit Button, the Whole Data Comes in URL Form?

To send form data in the URL, use the GET method in the <form> tag.

Example

<form action="submit.html" method="get">

Name:
<input type="text" name="username">

<br><br>

Email:
<input type="email" name="email">

<br><br>

<input type="submit" value="Submit">

</form>

After submission, the browser appends the form data to the URL in the following format: 

submit.html?username=Aayush&email=aayush@example.com
