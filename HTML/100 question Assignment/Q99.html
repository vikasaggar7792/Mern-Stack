What is the Main Reason or Main Advantage of the Label Tag?

The <label> tag provides a caption for form controls such as text fields, radio buttons, and checkboxes.

Advantages

  • Improves accessibility.
  • Clicking the label focuses or selects the associated input field.
  • Makes forms easier to understand.
  • Enhances the user experience.

Example

<label for="name">Name:</label>
<input type="text" id="name">
